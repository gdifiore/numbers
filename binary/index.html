Convert Decimal to Binary

Take number (we'll use 56) and follow these steps

56/2 = 28 with remainder of 0
28/2 = 14 with remainder of 0
14/2 = 7 with remainder of 0
7/2 = 3.5 with remainder of 1
then remove the half left over and continue dividing
3/2 = 1.5 with remainder of 1
remove half again
1/2 = .5 with remainder of 1
stop at 1/2

then write remainders backwords (1110002)

and if you convert that you get 5610

Convert Binary to Decimal

Take binary number (we'll use 1110002) and follow these steps
read binary numbers from right to left

the first number is 2^0 (1) the second is 2^1 (2) the third is 2^2 (4) and so on (for each position add another to the power of 2)

so lets take 111000 and convert it

32 16 8 4 2 1
1 1 1 0 0 0

so for each 0 you ignore the number above it and for each 1 you add the numbers above them together
32+16+8=56

and if we convert 111000 to decimal we get 5610
